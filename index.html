What is KASA?

The Korean American Student Association (KASA) is a cultural and social organization that aims to introduce and share Korean culture with all members of the UCSB community. At KASA, students come together to celebrate Korean culture, make lifelong friends, and create unforgettable college memories. Whether you're Korean, Korean-American, or just interested in Korean culture, everyone is welcome!
